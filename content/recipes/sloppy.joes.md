## Facts
*Total Time: 30 miuntes; Source: Matt*
## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp butter                      
- 0.25 tbsp chili powder                
- 1 tbsp garlic                      
- 1 pound ground beef                 
- 0.25 cups ketchup                     
- some mild hot sauce              
- 0.12 cups mustard                     
- 0.5 onion                       
- 0.5 tsp seasoned salt               
- 0.5 tbsp soy sauce                   
- 4 ounces tomato paste                
- 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce        
## Steps
1. Mix worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, chili powder, mustard, ketchup, tomato paste, seasoned salt, mild hot sauce to create a sauce.
*[chili powder: 0.25 tbsp; ketchup: 0.25 cups; mild hot sauce: some; mustard: 0.12 cups; seasoned salt: 0.5 tsp; soy sauce: 0.5 tbsp; tomato paste: 4 ounces; worcestershire sauce: 1 tbsp]*
2. Saute onion and garlic in butter.
*[butter: 1 tbsp; garlic: 1 tbsp; onion: 0.5]*
3. Add ground beef and cook, then drain grease.
*[ground beef: 1 pound]*
4. Serve.
