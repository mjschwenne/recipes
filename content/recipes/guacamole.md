## Facts
*Total Time: 15 minutes; Source: Matt*
## Ingredients
- 2 avocados                    
- some hot sauce                   
- some minced garlic               
- some scallions                   
- some seasoned salt               
- some tomatoes                    
## Cookware
- bowl
## Steps
1. Mash the avocados in a bowl.
*[avocados: 2]*
2. Add minced garlic, seasoned salt and scallions. Can also add tomatoes or hot sauce if desired.
