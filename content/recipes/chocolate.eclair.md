---
title: Chocolate Eclair
---
## Facts
*Total Time: 4 hours; Source: Matt*
## Ingredients
- 2 sleeves graham crackers             
- 3 cups milk                        
- 6 ounces pudding mix                 
- 8 ounces thawed cool whip            
## Cookware
- 13x9 pan
- large bowl
- mircowave
## Steps
1. Line the bottom of a 13x9 pan with graham crackers.
*[graham crackers: 2 sleeves]*
2. In a large bowl, combine pudding mix and milk, stirring well.
*[milk: 3 cups; pudding mix: 6 ounces]*
3. Stir thawed cool whip into the pudding mixture, then spread half of the mixture over the graham crackers.
*[thawed cool whip: 8 ounces]*
4. Top with an additional layer of graham crackers and the remaining pudding mixture.
5. mircowave frosting for 20 seconds and then pour or spread over the top.
6. Refrigerate until serving.
